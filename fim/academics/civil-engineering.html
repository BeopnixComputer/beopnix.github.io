Civil Engineering Program

MISSION

The mission of the Civil Engineering Program is to train professionals with an educational quality that meets the highest international levels of excellence on civil engineering areas forming students with the ability to plan, manage, design, model, build, operate and preserve infrastructure and services both nationally and internationally; with a global and multidisciplinary vision, being proactive as a leader and being aware of its participation in sustainable development and in harmony with university values. As well as developing and implementing technologies to solve economic, social and environmental problems, through creative and innovative proposals.

Faculty of Engineering, UABC.

VISION

In 2025 be one of the ten leading educational programs at the national level, internationally recognized by prestigious evaluating organizations thus, complying with the required quality standards and reach a consolidated link with the productive and academic sector.

Graduate Profile

Upon completion of this program, students should be able to:

  • Apply their knowledge related to a multidisciplinary approach oriented by mathematics, physics and chemistry.
  • Demonstrate a committed lifelong learning specialized in planning, management, design, modeling, construction, administration, operation and conservation.
  • Analyze, evaluate, interpret and present infrastructure projects and services, to solve problems.
  • Manage technologies that meet specific needs and contribute to the conservation of the sustainable environment, economic and social development in a national and international context.
  • Demonstrate oral and written communication skills, creativity and innovation, initiative, leadership, responsibility and proactive attitude in all areas of their professional practice.

Civil Engineer will be competent to:

  • Plan and manage infrastructure projects and services through the analysis of social, financial, technical needs and multidisciplinary networks for the development and innovation of the environment, with a critical and responsible attitude.
  • Design and model sustainable infrastructure projects using standards, techniques, specialized software and attending to the behavior of materials for urban and rural development with respect for the environment and a proactive attitude.
  • Execute the construction of civil works through construction management, current legislation and innovative procedures for urban and rural development with responsibility and respect for the environment.
  • Implement operation and conservation programs for existing infrastructure to ensure its proper functioning and useful life attending to technical, economic, environmental and social significance aspects with a proactive, responsible and efficient attitude.

Educational objectives

  • Civil Engineers plan, manage, design, model, build, administer, operate, and maintain infrastructure and services, addressing social, economic, environmental, and engineering problems at local, national and international levels.
  • Develop skills and attitudes to exert leadership in multidisciplinary groups and society, committing themselves to constant innovation with technological and territorial development.
  • Promote sustainable and competitive infrastructure projects development with a proactive, entrepreneurial, responsible, honest, efficient, and collaborative attitude.
  • Generate continuous improvement and capacity development habits, proper use of information technologies and manipulation of specialized software and professional tools.
  • Develop scientific research attitudes and skills as an improvement tool to attend the social and professional sector needs.
