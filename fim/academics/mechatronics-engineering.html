Mechatronics Engineering Program

MISSION

The mission of the Mechatronics Engineering Program is to train professionals in mechatronics engineering field with technical, research and transformative skills. Committed to the community and its environment to propose solutions to problems related to their profession in an effective and efficient way. In addition to being able to integrate into the same in social, cultural, political and productive aspects promoting or generating conditions to be achieved and always seeking compliance with institutional policies and commitments.

Faculty of Engineering, UABC.

VISION

In 2025, the Mechatronic Engineer educational program is widely recognized to be an educational program with national and international recognition where the activities and practices of students and teachers are developed with state-of-the-art technology and a high-level teacher training that promotes teachers and students’ values. Fostering the development of own technology to work projects with productive and social sectors.

Faculty of Engineering, UABC.

Admision Profile

Applicants to enter the Mechatronics Engineer educational program must have the following knowledge, skills, attitudes and values:

Knowledge:

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Computer science

Skills:

  • Analyze, interpret and solve problems
  • Verbal and written communicative skills
  • Teamwork

Attitudes:

  • Analytical thinking and tendency to optimization
  • Discipline, order, organization
  • Initiative to undertake research projects
  • Willingness and creative to solve problems
  • Leadership, responsibility and collaboration in teamwork
  • Open mindedness towards changes and scientific, technological and social development
  • Personal and professional improvement and updating search

Core Values:

  • Honesty
  • Respect
  • Responsibility
  • Tolerance
  • Commitment
  • Perseverance
  • Sense of belonging

Graduate Profile

Upon completion of this program, students should be able to:

  • The graduate of the Mechatronics Engineer educational program is an entrepreneurial professional leader, innovator, responsible with communication skills that promote teamwork.
  • Committed to industrial safety and the environment.
  • Manage a multidisciplinary approach capable of directing technological changes, committed to lifelong learning, specialized in the study, diagnosis, evaluation, design and administration of mechatronic systems.
  • Has the ability to solve productive processes problems efficiently and effectively and contribute to sustainable development in a national and international context.
  • Develop mechatronic systems through the design and integration of technologies complying with current technical and safety regulations to provide solutions to industrial problems with an attitude of leadership and professionalism preserving the environment with social responsibility.
  • Automate manufacturing processes through the application of instrumentation, control and supervision of systems to improve productivity and standardize the quality of products in an organized, efficient and punctual way.
  • Manage mechatronic projects through formulation, administration and evaluation to improve processes and optimize resources in a creative, innovative and collaborative way.

Educational objectives

  • Design and integrate mechatronic systems to provide optimal solutions to social and productive problems with ethics and environmental responsibility.
  • Mechatronic engineers develop projects to improve industrial processes by implementing and / or updating automation systems to standardize the quality of products and / or services through technical and financial analysis.
  • Mechatronic engineers understand and value the knowledge obtained through research and training in their discipline to update themselves and acquire emerging tools and techniques applicable to their areas of interest.
  • Mechatronic engineers integrate interdisciplinary work teams for the development of projects communicating effectively.

Occupational Field

Mechatronic Engineer may work in:

Private sector:

  • Health care sector
  • Educational and research Institutions
  • Electric and electronic sector
  • Automotive industry
  • Energies generation industries
  • Food sector
  • Agricultural sector industries

Public Sector:

  • Government agencies and decentralized agencies within the field of mechatronics
  • Trade and industrial development sectors
  • Parastatal industries
  • Communications and transport sector
  • Educational and research Institutions

Independent professionals:

  • Business consulting and assessment
