Bachelor degree in Computer Systems

MISSION

The mission of the Bachelor Degree in Computer Systems program is to prepare professionals in the area of Information and Communication Technologies, capable of developing and managing software products and implement and manage computer networks for productive development and the society benefit with leadership, entrepreneurial, honest and responsible behavior.

Faculty of Engineering, UABC.

VISION

In 2020, the Bachelor Degree in Computer Systems educational program has accreditations and certifications, leader in the training of Computer Systems Graduates. Recognized in a national and international productive field with at least one consolidated academic body linked to some productive and social sectors. An educational program that encourages entrepreneurial participation that allow students and graduates to apply their acquired knowledge in a multidisciplinary and interdisciplinary way.

Faculty of Engineering, UABC.

Admision Profile

Applicants to enter the Bachelor Degree in Computer Systems educational program must have the following knowledge, skills, attitudes and values:

Knowledge:

  • Mathematics
  • Accounting
  • Administration
  • Social and humanistic sciences

Skills:

  • Investigate and analyze information
  • New ideas generation
  • Basic software knowledge
  • Computer knowledge
  • Verbal and written communicative skills
  • Self learning

Attitudes:

  • Ethical and social commitment
  • Quality commitment
  • Interest in research and problem solving
  • Provision for teamwork
  • Be disciplined and organized to work
  • Willingness to carry out activities both in the administrative area and in the technical area

Core Values:

  • Honesty
  • Respect
  • Trust
  • Responsibility
  • Perseverance

Graduate Profile

Upon completion of this program, students should be able to:

  • Construct, manage and evaluate software products according to customer needs through the application of national and international software process models to ensure quality and introduce the best software engineering practices in organizations, with an attitude of commitment and willingness to work in interdisciplinary teams.
  • Design, implement and manage computer networks in different areas through the application of international standards. Plan, supervise, control of infrastructure and network services to propose quick and effective solutions in organizations at the local, state and regional level. National and international optimizing resources with creativity, responsibility and the environment protection.
  • Manage information in technology areas proactively to organizations through efficient use of communication methods and the application of technical and methodological knowledge to optimize the resources of companies contributing to the achievement of their objectives and the economy development of the country with an entrepreneurial attitude, willingness to work as a team, leadership, responsibility and honesty.

Educational objectives

  • Apply acquired knowledge in analysis, design, implementation, evaluation and management of software projects through the use of national and international models of software processes, programming tools and emerging technologies to generate quality software products in organizations.
  • Manage computer infrastructures applying national and international norms and standards, to offer connectivity solutions with scalability, robustness, and security.
  • Ethically and responsibly manage areas of information technology to streamline processes and optimize resources of organizations through teamwork performed with values, social responsibility and aware of their commitment to sustainable development and technological progress.
  • Communicate effectively orally and in writing with multidisciplinary and multicultural groups to collaborate and spread their ideas and knowledge.

Occupational Field

Bachelor Degree in Computer Systems engineers may work in:

Private/Public sector:

  • Companies that require information technology support
  • Administration of IT units covering software and computing areas.
  • Software project management.
  • Analysis, design, development and implementation of software applications.
  • Resources and budgets coordination

Independent professionals:

  • Providing consulting and advice in the area of information technology
  • Entrepreneur of the software industry at national and international level
