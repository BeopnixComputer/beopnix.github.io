Electronic Engineering Program

MISSION

The mission of the Electronic Engineering Program is to train rain human resources of excellence in the field of electronic engineering, competitive in the national and international scene, committed to society and its institution, capable of generating and applying knowledge, as well as developing skills for solving problems in the discipline. In order to improve society's life quality using the engineering state art within a context of values and in harmony to the environment.

Engineers that recognize and practice continuous updating in their specialization area, using scientific knowledge, technological, humanistic in a higher position with a multidisciplinary approach, as well as the foundations, techniques, methods of bioengineering and quality methodologies.

According with current regulations to solve problems in the field of health and insurance of quality in industrial processes, contributing to sustainable development, achieving continuous improvement in production, optimizing technological resources for health sector companies.

Faculty of Engineering, UABC.

VISION

In 2029, the Electronic Engineer educational program is a consolidated program that trains high-quality professionals, generates knowledge and develops technology that is transferred to the productive sector. It is relevant, innovative and recognized by external organizations, which position it as one of the best in Mexico.

  • Algebra
  • Trigonometry
  • Analytic Geometry
  • Physics

Skills:

  • Verbal and written communicative skills
  • Reading skills
  • Interpretation of physical phenomena
  • Problem solving
  • Organization

Attitudes:

  • Proactive
  • Perseverance

Core Values:

  • Honesty
  • Respect
  • Responsibility

Graduate Profile

Upon completion of this program, students should be able to:

  • Electronic engineer is a responsible professional with a multidisciplinary approach, committed to continuous learning. Has knowledge, skills and attitudes to plan, design, implement, maintain, manage and evaluate electronic systems that are applied in the solution of engineering problems, thereby contributing to the satisfaction of needs for sustainable development in economic, environmental and social nationally and internationally contexts.
  • Formulate, manage and evaluate electronics projects through the application of electronic engineering knowledge, methodologies, techniques and tools for the optimal management of project resources with a professional attitude in an organized way and with a teamwork culture.
  • Design and integrate electronic systems through the analysis of the corresponding requirements and the application of methodologies to solve electronic engineering problems in a responsible manner with creative attitude and a sense of permanent training.
  • Construct, implement and validate electronic systems by complying with the corresponding technical specifications and standards for solving problems in electronic engineering with ethical responsibility, teamwork and effective communication.
  • Operate, maintain and manage electronic systems through operation procedures, maintenance and current regulations for the efficient use of systems with ethical responsibility a sense of permanent training and effective communication.

Educational objectives

  • Identify, formulate and solve complex engineering problems applying the principles of basic science and engineering.
  • Apply, analyze and synthesize engineering design processes that result in projects that meet specified needs.
  • Develop and conduct adequate experimentation; analyze and interpret data and use engineering judgment to draw conclusions.
  • Communicate effectively with different audiences.
  • Recognize their ethical and professional responsibilities in relevant to engineering situations and make informed judgments that consider the impact of engineering solutions in a global, economic, environmental and social context.
  • Recognize the continuing need for additional knowledge and have the ability to locate, evaluate, integrate and apply this knowledge appropriately.
  • Work effectively in teams to set goals, plan tasks, meet deadlines and analyze risks.

  • Parastatal Institutions

Independent professionals:

  • Technology advisor
  • Projects development
