Bioengineering Program

MISSION

The mission of the Bioengineering Program is to train competent engineers in the biomedical and bio-industrial area. Responsible, creative, supportive, participatory with an entrepreneurial, innovative and leadership attitude. Engineers committed to society, capable of effectively communicate in verbal and written issues related to their professional practice; to generate equipment and instruments for biomedical and biotechnological use; to incorporate and integrate technological and information systems for biomedical and bio-industrial use.

Engineers that recognize and practice continuous updating in their specialization area, using scientific knowledge, technological, humanistic in a higher position with a multidisciplinary approach, as well as the foundations, techniques, methods of bioengineering and quality methodologies.

According with current regulations to solve problems in the field of health and insurance of quality in industrial processes, contributing to sustainable development, achieving continuous improvement in production, optimizing technological resources for health sector companies.

Faculty of Engineering, UABC.

VISION

In 2030, the Bioengineer program is a widely recognized program, dynamic and committed to society in the generation of professionals, trained in an environment of high scientific and technological quality in a high civic and ethical level in which students respond assertively to situations in their personal, social and professional life through active participation in teaching processes and innovative research in the bioengineering area.

Faculty of Engineering, UABC.

Admision Profile

Applicants to enter the Bioengineer educational program must have the following knowledge, skills, attitudes and values:

Knowledge:

  • Biology
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Mathematics
  • Computer science
  • English

Skills:

  • Verbal and written communicative skills
  • Teamwork
  • Study habits
  • Organization

Attitudes:

  • Continuous learning interest
  • Discipline
  • Community service
  • Sense of order
  • Proactive

Core Values:

  • Life and environment respect
  • Respect for their peers and the institution
  • Honesty and commitment

Graduate Profile

Upon completion of this program, students should be able to:

  • Design and generate equipment, devices and materials for biomedical, biotechnological and environmental use through the application of theoretical-practical foundations of bioengineering and international quality standards to achieve continuous improvement of technological and economic resources for health sector companies. Demonstrate collaborative attitude and social responsibility with emphasis to the environment well-being.
  • Propose and manage physical spaces for biomedical technological and computer systems. Applying bioengineering techniques and tools to biomedical, biotechnological and industrial functions in accordance with current regulations. In order to improve health and insurance in industrial processes quality with honesty and the best attitude towards interdisciplinary work.
  • Design and implement strategies for the generation of bioprocesses through fundamentals techniques, bioengineering methods and biotechnological resources. Manage the prevention and resolution of environmental problems and contribute to sustainable development with an innovative, committed and tolerant attitude.
  • Manage human, material and financial resources in the design, manufacture, marketing, equipment of bioengineering products and services through the application of administrative processes to contribute to the development of organizations and the creation of new productive spaces with leadership, ethics, entrepreneurship, creativity and responsibility.

Educational objectives

  • Ensure that Bioengineering students are capable to contribute to the development of the productive and social sector of the region and the country. Bioengineering students have a wide range of knowledge, skills, attitudes and values to solve emerging problems and solve technological needs with efficiency and respect for the environment.
  • Promote sustainable development through bioengineering innovation with professionals in design and interdisciplinary projects management that address productive and social sector problems.
  • Bioengineering professionals are capable to identify opportunities and improve their skills to work in emerging areas of society and the productive sector and contribute to the economic development of the region and the country.
  • Contribute to the manufacturing systematization of commercial processes of products for biotechnological and biomedical purposes with bioengineers highly trained to meet the industrial and health care sector’s needs.

Occupational Field

Bioengineers may work in:

Private sector:

  • Health care sector
  • Educational and research Institutions
  • Medical equipment and materials manufacturing industry
  • Biotechnology industry
  • Environmental industry
  • Service companies

Public Sector:

  • Government
  • Health care Institutions
  • Educational and research Institutions
  • Environment and use of natural resources Institutions
  • Agricultural sector companies

Independent professionals:

  • Advice and training of personnel in the area of bioengineering
  • Provides advisory services and training of personnel in the bioengineering area
  • Bioengineers work on their own company
