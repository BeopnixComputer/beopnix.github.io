Mechanical Engineering Program

MISSION

The mission of the Mechanical Engineering Program is to train professionals in the area of Mechanical Engineering with required updated knowledge, values, skills and attitudes responding to productive environment needs and with a high commitment to society in concordance with the environment through teaching processes, dynamic and modern learning. Stablishing highly qualified and competent personnel, state-of-the-art technology laboratories and study plans attached to the bases of competence.

Engineers that recognize and practice continuous updating in their specialization area, using scientific knowledge, technological, humanistic in a higher position with a multidisciplinary approach, as well as the foundations, techniques, methods of bioengineering and quality methodologies.

According with current regulations to solve problems in the field of health and insurance of quality in industrial processes, contributing to sustainable development, achieving continuous improvement in production, optimizing technological resources for health sector companies.

Faculty of Engineering, UABC.

VISION

In 2025, the Mechanical Engineer educational program is widely recognized with a national and international status that corresponds to a dynamic globalization environment through leadership and competence with updated study plans, modern laboratories and certified teaching staff. Mechanical engineers have competitive levels and profiles and their work reflects on its impact on the industry and on society itself in an effective way. Mechanical engineers actively participate in outreach projects that allow scientific research to solve problems, as well as the insertion of teachers and students in an efficient, dynamic, pertinent and transcendent teaching-learning process.

Faculty of Engineering, UABC.

Admision Profile

Applicants to enter the Mechanical Engineer educational program must have the following knowledge, skills, attitudes and values:

Knowledge:

  • Algebra
  • Trigonometry
  • Differential and integral calculus
  • Physics
  • Chemistry

Skills:

  • Critical thinking
  • Observation
  • Interpret and analyze problems
  • Computer equipment proper use
  • Instrumentation and laboratory equipment management
  • Verbal and written communicative skills
  • Teamwork

Attitudes:

  • Interest in science
  • Discipline
  • Initiative
  • Leadership
  • Creativity

Core Values:

  • Tolerance and respect for national and foreign ideologies, preferences and cultures.
  • Responsibility to fulfill academic tasks and activities.
  • Empathy with the social and economic situation of the environment.
  • Honesty

Graduate Profile

Upon completion of this program, students should be able to:

  • Identify, formulate and solve complex engineering problems applying the principles of basic science and engineering.
  • Apply, analyze, and synthesize engineering design processes that result in projects that meet specified needs.
  • Develop and conduct adequate experimentation; analyze and interpret data using engineering judgment to draw conclusions.
  • Communicate effectively with different audiences.
  • Recognize their ethical and professional responsibilities in relevant situations in order to make informed judgments considering the impact of engineering solutions in a global, economic, environmental and social context.
  • Recognize the continuing need for additional knowledge and have the ability to locate, evaluate, integrate and apply this knowledge appropriately.
  • Work effectively in teams to set goals, plan tasks, meet deadlines and analyze risks.

Educational objectives

  • Analyze, design and optimize mechanical, thermal, hydraulic or pneumatic systems.
  • Generate and analyze data.
  • Develop manufacturing process
  • Integrate and lead effective work teams.

Occupational Field

Mechanical Engineer may work in:

Private sector:

  • Transformation industry as a design and manufacturing engineer
  • Metal-mechanical extractive industry as a process engineer
  • Electricity sector in the mechanical area
  • Power generation plants
  • Steam production areas
  • Conduction and condensation
  • Hydraulic, pneumatic and turbomachinery systems
  • Industrial, commercial and domestic refrigeration and air conditioning services and installations
  • In the area of services and installations of industrial and commercial fluid pumping and conduction systems.
  • Engineering design department

Public Sector:

  • Research centers as researcher in the implementation of projects of new energy sources, mechanical design and manufacturing processes.
  • Parastatal companies

Independent professionals:

  • As an independent professional
  • Advisory activity in the domain area
  • Develop their own product or service company
