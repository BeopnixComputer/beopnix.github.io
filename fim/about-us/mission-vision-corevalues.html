Mission

Train professionals in the engineering area in a undergraduate and postgraduate level, complying with the best standards of educational quality, being capable of providing optimal solutions to problems in the field, in harmony with university values and seeking social wellness. In addition, carry out basic and applied research and promote technological innovation and industry linkage.

Vision

In 2025, Engineering faculty is widely recognized for being a socially responsible academic unit, which contributes with opportunity, relevance and the best quality standards to the comprehensive training of professionals in engineering areas. Its educational programs are accredited by different national and international evaluation Institutions. It promotes, generates, applies, disseminates and transfers knowledge. Boost innovation as well as strengthen industry linkage and research which turns it in one of the best engineering schools in Mexico and Latin America.

Core Values

The FIM shares UABC fundamental values as a result of a survey conducted through the FIM community, the following results were obtained:

  • Honesty
  • Trust
  • Humility
  • Democracy
  • Loyalty
  • Solidarity
  • Respect
  • Responsability
  • Justice
  • Freedom
  • Perseverance
